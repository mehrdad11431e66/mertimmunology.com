Advanced Immunology Research Center

Our research center is dedicated to advancing the understanding of immune system functions and developing innovative solutions for immunological disorders. We combine cutting-edge technology with interdisciplinary approaches to tackle the most pressing challenges in immunology and biomedical science.

Research Areas

Our Specialized Research Domains

Immunogenetics

Exploring the genetic basis of immune responses and autoimmune disorders through advanced genomic technologies.

Vaccine Development

Researching novel vaccine technologies and delivery systems for emerging infectious diseases.

Biomedical Research

Investigating molecular mechanisms of disease and immunity at cellular and molecular levels.

Drug Formulation

Developing novel drug delivery systems and immunomodulatory compounds for therapeutic applications.

Agricultural Biotech

Applying immunological principles to enhance plant resistance and agricultural productivity.

Scientific Education

Providing training and education programs in immunology and biomedical research methodologies.
