About Me

Independent Researcher in Immunology & Biomedical Science

I am Mehrdad Etemad, an independent researcher with an unshakable passion for biomedical science. My journey is fueled by curiosity and a commitment to unraveling the mysteries of the immune system, medical genetics, and pharmaceutical advancements.

Every piece of research, every article, and every discussion shared here is a step toward a future where science serves humanity—better vaccines, safer medicines, healthier food, and sustainable agriculture.
